5-Ingredient Salmon Recipe

Description

This easy salmon recipe is a delicious way to prepare fresh fillets using just a few ingredients from your pantry.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 4(6 ounce) fillets salmon
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 lemon wedges

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Stir garlic powder, basil, and salt together in a small bowl.
  3. Rub the mixture evenly over salmon.
  4. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
  5. Add salmon and cook until browned and flaky, about 5 minutes per side.
  6. Serve salmon with lemon wedges and side of choice.

Pan-Fried Shrimp

Description

This pan-fried shrimp recipe features small bay shrimp cooked on the stove with

simple seasoning.

Ingredients

  • 8 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 1 pound peeled and deveined bay shrimp
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice, divided
  • 1 pinch garlic salt, or to tase
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Melt 6 tablespoons butter in a large skillet over high heat.
  2. add shrimp;cook and stir in hot butter until browned, about 2 minutes
  3. Reduce heat to medium-low
  4. stir in 1 1/2 teaspoons lemon juice, garlic salt, salt, and pepper
  5. cook until heated through, about 1 minute
  6. Remove from heat; stir in remaining 2 tablespoons butter and 1 1/2 teaspoons lemon juice

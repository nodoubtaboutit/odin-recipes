Two-Ingredient Peanut Butter Fudge

Description

This two-ingredient peanut butter fudge is the easiest recipe you will

ever find! And it is made in the microwave.

Ingredients

  • waxed paper
  • 1(18 ounce) jar peanut butter
  • 1(12 ounce) container whipped cream cheese frosting

Directions

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Line an 8-in square pan with waxed paper; set aside.
  3. Spoon peanut butter and frosting into a microwave-save bowl.
  4. Heat in the microwave on high for 30 seonds.
  5. Stir mixture and heat again for 30 seconds.
  6. Stir again until thoroughly blended.
  7. Pour mixture into the prepared baking pan.
  8. Refrigerate until chilled, about an hour.
  9. Remove from fridge and cut into bite-sized squares.
